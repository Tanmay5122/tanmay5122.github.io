SCORVEN: Smart Crowd Observation and Response Vehicle for Emergency Navigation

In an era where public safety and efficient emergency response are more critical than ever, SCORVEN emerges as a groundbreaking solution. SCORVEN, short for Smart Crowd Observation and Response Vehicle for Emergency Navigation, is a drone-powered system designed to intelligently monitor crowd density and facilitate real-time decision-making during emergencies.
SCORVEN was developed to address a growing challenge faced by law enforcement, disaster response teams, and public event organizers — understanding and managing dense human gatherings in real time. Traditional methods often rely on fixed CCTV infrastructure, which lacks the flexibility and aerial perspective required for dynamic environment

How SCORVEN Works

SCORVEN integrates advanced AI algorithms with drone mobility to detect and respond to dense crowds autonomously. Unlike other systems, SCORVEN does not rely on CSRNet (a typical crowd density estimation model); instead, it uses lightweight yet powerful object detection models like YOLOv8s to identify and cluster human figures efficiently.
Real-Time Crowd Detection Using the YOLOv8s model, SCORVEN detects individuals and analyzes crowd density with remarkable speed and accuracy.

Dynamic Heatmap Visualization

The system generates intuitive heatmaps that highlight crowded zones and cluster formations, helping authorities identify potential risks early.

Autonomous Navigation

SCORVEN isn’t just a passive observer. It can autonomously navigate to areas of concern using clustering logic and obstacle avoidance, ensuring quick access to high-density zones.

Emergency Routing Assistance

During evacuations or public events, SCORVEN aids in guiding people through less crowded pathways, enhancing emergency navigation capabilities.

Manual Override and Web Interface

Operators can monitor the live drone feed and receive alerts through a responsive Flask-based web interface, with manual control available in critical situations.

Why the World Needs SCORVEN

Disaster Management

During earthquakes, floods, or stampedes, SCORVEN can assess crowd congestion and direct emergency services.

Public Event Monitoring

Concerts, festivals, and sports events benefit from aerial crowd oversight without the need for expensive infrastructure.

Law Enforcement

SCORVEN supports real-time surveillance during protests or rallies, helping maintain order while respecting civil boundaries.

What Sets SCORVEN Apart?

SCORVEN stands out as a purpose-built autonomous drone that redefines what’s possible with compact, cost-effective aerial intelligence. Unlike typical drones focused on FPV or recreational use, SCORVEN is engineered purely for function—real-time surveillance, anomaly detection, and autonomous decision-making. It uses an onboard Arducam IMX519 PDAF&CDAF Autofocus Camera Module for Raspberry Pi, NVIDIA® Jetson AGX Orin/Orin Nano/Orin NX to process visual data directly on the edge, enabling it to identify pedestrians, track anomalies, and respond without relying on cloud computation or external processing units. This ensures both speed and privacy.
The drone's design abandons traditional FPV streaming, replacing it with a laser focus on mission-critical data collection. It integrates GPS for geolocation, Lidar for spatial awareness, and a robust compute stack using Raspberry Pi—creating a system that can navigate, observe, and react entirely on its own. Every component is handpicked for maximum value, achieving a level of autonomy and performance typically reserved for high-end systems, but at a fraction of the cost.
What makes SCORVEN truly different is its intention. It’s not just flying tech—it’s a civic tool. Designed to aid government bodies, disaster response teams, and urban surveillance operations, it balances stealth with precision, staying out of sight while providing real-time intelligence. SCORVEN doesn’t seek to entertain; it exists to serve, protect, and inform—autonomously, intelligently, and relentlessly.
